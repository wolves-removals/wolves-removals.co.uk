Moving Checklist

Your stress-free guide to moving home — from Wolves Removals, Sussex

A smooth move starts with a good plan.

Tick off each task as you go. Need a hand with any of it? Our friendly, fully insured Sussex team can pack, move and store it all — call 01903 893731 or get a free quote at wolves-removals.co.uk.

6–8 Weeks Before

  • Confirm your moving date with Wolves Removals
  • Book your free survey (video or in-home)
  • Declutter room by room — donate, sell, recycle
  • Order packing materials or book a packing service
  • Notify your landlord or estate agent
  • Research schools and doctors in your new area

4 Weeks Before

  • Confirm your removals quote and dates
  • Start packing non-essential items
  • Notify utilities — gas, electric, water, broadband
  • Arrange mail redirection with Royal Mail
  • Update banks, insurers and your employer
  • Register to vote at your new address

2 Weeks Before

  • Confirm parking and access at both properties
  • Run down fridge and freezer food
  • Arrange childcare or pet care for moving day
  • Label every box clearly by room
  • Back up important computer files
  • Cancel or transfer local deliveries

1 Week Before

  • Pack a “first-night essentials” box
  • Confirm final details with Wolves Removals
  • Defrost and dry out the freezer
  • Dismantle flat-pack furniture (or ask us to)
  • Charge phones and gather all chargers
  • Keep keys, cash and documents together

Moving Day

  • Be ready when the Wolves team arrives
  • Take final meter readings (old & new home)
  • Do a last walk-through — loft, cupboards, garden
  • Keep valuables and documents with you
  • Check nothing is left behind
  • Hand over keys to the agent or new occupier

After the Move

  • Check your inventory against the boxes
  • Locate the stopcock, fuse box and smoke alarms
  • Update your driving licence and V5C
  • Update the electoral roll and council tax
  • Say hello to your new neighbours
  • Leave Wolves Removals a review!
