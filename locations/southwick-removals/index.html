Preparing for Your Southwick Move

A little preparation makes a big difference to how smoothly your Southwick removal runs. We recommend starting six to eight weeks ahead where you can: confirm your dates, book your survey and begin a room-by-room declutter so you only pay to move what you actually want in your new West Sussex home. Anything you are unsure about, our team is happy to advise on — from what can be stored to what is best left behind.

Packing is usually the biggest job of any Southwick move. You can pack yourself using our boxes and materials, or hand it over entirely to our full packing service. Either way, we strongly recommend professional fragile packing for glassware, mirrors, artwork and anything sentimental. Label every box clearly by room, and keep a separate “first night” box of essentials so you are not hunting through cartons on your first evening in Southwick.

In the final week before your Southwick move, let your utility providers, bank and the West Sussex council know you are moving, arrange mail redirection, and run down your fridge and freezer ready for defrosting. It also pays to plan access at both properties — parking, narrow lanes, stairs or lifts — which we will already have noted at your survey so your Southwick crew arrives ready to work. If your completion dates do not quite line up, our secure containerised storage bridges the gap for as long as you need.

On moving day itself, our trained, fully insured Southwick team handles the heavy lifting, protects your floors and furniture as standard, and places everything in the right room at the other end — unpacking as much as you would like. To make your move easier still, you can download our free essential moving guide and work through it at your own pace.
